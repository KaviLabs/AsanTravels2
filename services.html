ITINERARY

12 Nights / 13 Days East & North Tour

  1. Day 01: Airport to Negombo/Halawatha – Beach stay hotel
  2. Day 02: Leisure day on the beach
  3. Day 03: Negombo to Anuradhapura via Wilpattu National Park
  4. Day 04: Anuradhapura sightseeing → Transfer to Jaffna
  5. Day 05: Explore Jaffna – Jaffna Fort, Nagadeepa, Market, Temple, Ice Cream
  6. Day 06: Jaffna to Trincomalee – Beach relaxation
  7. Day 07: Explore Trinco – Pigeon Island, Kovil, Fort Frederick, Cemetery, Hot Springs
  8. Day 08: Trincomalee to Habarana – Minneriya National Park
  9. Day 09: Habarana – Sigiriya Rock, Village tour, Ayurveda, Cultural show
  10. Day 10: Habarana to Kandy – Dambulla, Spice Garden, Tooth Temple, Shopping
  11. Day 11: Kandy to Haputale – Tea Factory, Ramboda Falls, Train ride
  12. Day 12: Haputale to Udawalawa – National Park, Elephant Transit Home
  13. Day 13: Udawalawa to Beach Transfer – Stilt Fishing, Turtle Hatchery, Madu River
  14. Final: Airport transfer provided
